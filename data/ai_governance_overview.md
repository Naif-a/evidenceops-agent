# AI Governance Overview

## Risk Classification

AI systems should be classified according to their potential impact. Low-risk systems provide informational assistance and cannot make consequential decisions. High-impact systems can affect access, safety, finances, employment, or legal rights.

## Human Approval

High-impact actions require explicit human approval. Approval must apply to one specific request and must not automatically transfer to another session.

## Accountability

Every consequential action must record the time, action, request identifier, user approval status, and outcome. The organization must be able to reconstruct how a decision was produced.

## Evidence Requirements

Research agents must retrieve evidence before making factual claims. Responses should separate verified evidence, model inference, recommendations, uncertainty, and missing information.
